About Us

Welcome To FIPI

The Federation of Indian Petroleum Industry Student (FIPI) was founded by distinguished members representing the entire spectrum of the hydrocarbon industry and was named Petrotech Student Chapter. The Governing Council of the society is well represented by the multinational, private sector and major public sector oil companies.

The society functions under the ambit of Memorandum of Association and its Bye-laws. The primary objective was to create a knowledge sharing environment between the industry players and the PETROTECH STUDENT CHAPTER (now, FIPI SC), being the first of its kind.

UPES FIPI

Since its inception in 2007, the UPES FIPI Student Chapter (erstwhile known as UPES Petrotech Student Chapter) has been instrumental in training the novices, while polishing the students pursuing advanced levels in academics, and has earned tremendous respect because of this reason. In pursuit of the same, 12 chapters across the nation were established, with UPES FIPI SC being awarded “The Best Chapter” award for several years along with “The Best Presentation” award. Recently, the chapter has been awarded with the Best Presentation Award at the 8th FIPI Convention held in 2018.

UPES FIPI Student Chapter is the oldest student chapter in UPES.

We have conducted a splendorous chain of events including several weeks like Chemical Week, English Week and numerous other events. We have had a huge successful winter colloquium for the academic session 2017-18, JIGYASA which was held in November 2017. We have had Dr. Rafat Jamaal (Humboldt Fellow, Scientist G and Head, Biostratigraphy Division, Wadia Institute of Himalayan Geology and Associate Advisor) and Dr. Anil Kumar (Ex-Deputy General Manager, Reservoir and ONGC) as key guest speakers. Apart from these lectures, we had conducted four major events successfully and saw huge participation of students from the university. The events were ‘Transcendence’ which was the paper presentation competition, ‘Envisage’, the poster presentation competition, ‘Knock Down Buzz’, a symposium cum quiz event and ‘Petro hunt’, the treasure hunt.

This year the UPES FIPI Student Chapter is back bigger and better and we want you to join us in this year’s journey of curiosity. This year we bring you an opportunity to be part of an exclusive sponsorship club and sponsor JIGYASA, Powered by HPCL, helping us in making it a phenomenal success and delivering happiness and the gift of knowledge to students happy and proud of taking home the knowledge. JIGYASA would be held during 5-6th April, 2019.
