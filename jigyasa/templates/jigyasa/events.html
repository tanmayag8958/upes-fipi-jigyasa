TRANSCENDENCE

“A person’s creativity is only bounded by the limits of his vessel, not those of his mind.”
Each individual retains the flair of innovation within themselves, regardless of its measure, a fragment of their conscience which urges them to incept and improve relentlessly. Yet their wheel of progress remains subdued, not due to their lack of potential, but due to the sparingly available opportunities fit for them to unleash their aptitude.
With the motive of bringing together such talent and unraveling their imagination, UPES FIPI Student Chapter, unveils “Transcendence”, the premier technical paper presentation event among its peers, hosted under the mantle of the Chapter’s flagship academic colloquium of this session ”JIGYASA”. This event promises a gathering of participants from various accomplished institutions across the nation, convening to share their unique thoughts over the current trends in the Oil and Gas domain, exclusively in the upstream and downstream sector. “Transcendence” seeks out the inquisitive minds ready to outpour the world with their original prospects, and aims to provide the best presentation platform for their minds.

KNOCKDOWN BUZZ

“The best teamwork comes from men, who are working independently towards one goal in unison.”
Just like it takes 2 hands to make a clap, it takes two flints to light a fire, each one playing a vital role to achieve a simple goal - something which is impossible to achieve alone. Keeping up with this theme, UPES FIPI Student Chapter brings to you “The Knockdown Buzz”, a cumulative event which shall test the participant’s teamwork and adaptability in a team of 4.
Consisting of two rounds, the first round will test the basic aptitude of a team with the means of a quiz and a group discussion conducted simultaneously. Teams qualified will be promoted to the second round where two selected members of each team shall indulge in an invigorating quiz based on their handpicked topics, while the other two will participate in a symposium where one of them will be part of a panel. All the formed panels will be given a common topic related to the Oil & Gas industry to express their perspectives as per the roles appointed to them (i.e. marketing analyst, drilling engineer etc.)
The team with the best cumulative score will be adjudged the winning team.
So, gear up with your words and skills to sink in the ocean of debates and knowledge.
